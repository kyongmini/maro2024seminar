2024-2 MARO ACADEMIC SEMINAR

Undergraduates from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Kyung Hee University

Introduction

Welcome to the 2024-2 MARO Academic Seminar!

This seminar is hosted by MARO from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Kyung Hee University.

The seminar aims to (1) help freshmen and sophomores explore career paths and find motivation, and (2) provide juniors and seniors with opportunities for career exploration, academic study, or to introduce their research fields to undergraduates.

The seminar will take place every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:30 PM on November 5th, 7th, 12th, 14th, and 19th, featuring a total of 19 presenters.
